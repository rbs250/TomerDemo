Meet Tomer

"Style is a reflection of your attitude and your personality."

After honing my skills in leading salons in London and Los Angeles, I returned to Israel to bring international precision to Tel Aviv.

I have had the privilege of styling celebrities (including Kim Kardashian) and serving as Eden Harel's private hairdresser for four years.

Community: Proud volunteer with 'Olam' and provider of haircuts for the homeless.
